Southern Dunes Golf and Country Club

Opened in 1993, Southern Dunes is an upscale daily fee course that continues to receive numerous awards from the golf industry. The charm and appeal of the club, comes from the friendly staff and unique characteristics of the course.

www.southerndunes.com

 

 

 

This dramatic hillside layout stretches to over 7,200 yards and features 100ft. of elevation changes, not typically found in Central Florida.

Five sets of tees welcome golfers of all skill levels to enjoy risk/reward opportunities, through extensive mounding and strategically placed bunkers. The design is enhanced by the well maintained playing surfaces.

Southern Dunes Policies

  • Southern Dunes does not allow metal spikes. Soft spikes are required all year and can be purchased at the golf shop.

  • All food and beverages must be purchased on site.

  • All players must register in the golf shop before play.

  • Proper golf attire is required. Tank tops and cut-offs are not allowed on the course.

  • Players must be 18 or older to rent a golf cart.

  • All players must have their own bag and set of clubs while playing. Rental clubs are available in the Golf Shop.
