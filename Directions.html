DRIVING DIRECTIONS  to PatricksVilla


Directions from the North / Orlando International Airport via I 4 / US 27

 

From Orlando International Airport (MCO)

  •  
  • Leaving the car rental car park, follow the signs for “Exit SOUTH”
  • Take Hwy 417 (Greenway) toll road to I-4 West / Tampa. Ideally you should have US$ 1.00 bills and/or quarters to pay at the toll booth.
  • Once on the I-4 proceed to exit #55 to US Hwy 27 and exit to your right
  • Go south on Hwy 27 (right at the traffic lights)
  • Proceed south on US 27 for approximately 7 miles.
  • As soon as you see a McDonalds on your right, get into the left lane and turn left at the lights. Another landmark you won't be able to miss is the 24h Super-Wal Mart that is outside the Southern Dunes Golf and Country Club.
  • After you turned left you should have the Wal-Mart on your left and a Win-Dixie and a Dollar Tree shop on your right.
  • Keep going for a few yards and you will see the 24h security gate.
  • Please present your booking confirmation (a print out of this email) to the guard and let him know that you are staying for X amount of days. He will issue you with a temporary ID that should be displayed in the car whenever your car is parked on the premises.
  • After you have passed the security gate, continue to follow the Southern Dunes Blvd until you see a very short road on your right. This is Forest Hills Lane. Turn right into Forest Hills Lane and immediately turn right again.
  • Follow the road down, all the way to the end, 1634 Forest Hills Lane, PatricksVilla, is right in front of you.

Relax and enjoy your stay

Directions from South / Miami via SR836 / SR826 / US 27

 

From Miami International Airport (MIA)
 

  • Leaving any of the car rental lots, follow the signs for SR 836 West (to Florida Turnpike)

  • Once on the SR836 in westerly direction look out for SR 826 North.

  • The exit follows shortly after you have left the airport behind you on your right hand side

  • Keep going north for approximately 4 miles on the SR826 until you come to the exit of US 27 / Okeechobee Road

  • Leave the SR826 at the US27 and go in north/westerly direction. As a point of reference, you should see a  Super Wal Mart to your right.

  • Now keep going for approximately. 200 miles, always staying on the US27, it couldn't be easier.

  • When you get to Haines City you will see yet another Super Wal Mart on your right.

  • Turn right onto the Wal-Mart drive into Southern Dunes Boulevard.

  • After you turned right you should have the Wal-Mart on your left and a Win-Dixie and a Dollar Tree shop on your right.

  • Keep going for a few yards and you will see the 24h security gate of the Southern  Dunes Golf and Country Club in front of you.

  • Please present your booking confirmation (a print out of this email) to the guard and let him know that you are staying for X amount of days. He will issue you with a temporary ID that should be displayed in the car whenever your car is parked  on the premises.

  • After you have passed the security gate, continue to follow the Southern Dunes Blvd until you see a very short road on your right. This is Forest Hills Lane. Turn right into Forest Hills Lane and immediately turn right again.
  • Follow the road down, all the way to the end, 1634 Forest Hills Lane, PatricksVilla, is right in front of you.
