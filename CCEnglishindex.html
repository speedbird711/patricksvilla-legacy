  Welcome to PatricksVilla in Cape Coral
Completed in July 2023, PatricksVilla CC offers luxury accommodations with 4 bedrooms and 4 en-suite bathrooms.

Our heated saltwater pool is not only oversized, it also features a whirlpool/hot tub, water loungers on the sun shelf, a full-length pool bench, and a 6-meter-long infinity edge. The view of the boat dock and the canal is simply stunning.

Our house is located in the modern northwest of Cape Coral, an area that was developed only a few years ago and therefore consists predominantly of new and modern homes. A safe and beautiful environment crisscrossed by a freshwater and saltwater canal system.

PatricksVilla is located on a wide and healthy saltwater canal where you can see fish and manatees, among other species. With our kayaks, which we provide free of charge, you can quickly reach the mangroves of Matlachee and the Gulf of Mexico. If you want to explore the neighborhood from the water, there's probably no better way than by boat through the canals. It's not for nothing that Cape Coral is called the Venice of the USA.

Every detail of the house was personally planned and selected by us, including the pool and boat dock. The house was furnished with modern features and offers everything you need for an exceptional vacation. We were fully involved from the planning stage through to the construction phase, ensuring that modern touches were combined with every comfort.

Cleanliness and quality are our top priority. Our guests always receive personal attention, and both houses are almost always cleaned and prepared by us for the next guests. You never speak to a rental agency, but always with us, the owners.

If you have any special requests, please just ask.

The Pool, the Whirlpool and the Terrace



The heated saltwater pool with whirlpool (chlorine-free and particularly skin-friendly) offers a fantastic view of the picturesque saltwater canal.

The water surface of our infinity pool is approximately
11 x 4.5 meters, and the pool can, of course, also be illuminated.

There is also a water sun lounger area measuring approximately 3 x 3 meters, which includes two small fountains and two loungers.

The two water loungers are of course at your disposal, as are the six additional loungers, which can be set up either under cover or next to the pool.

An infrared gas grill and a dining table for 8 people promise unforgettable evenings by the pool with a cool drink and a romantic, freshly grilled dinner.

The comfortable seating area and the table equipped with a gas fireplace allow you to end a fantastic day in style.




For your health and well-being, we have installed the latest pool cleaning technology.

Our pool is disinfected using a natural, chemical process. Only salt and very few chemicals are required. The environment thanks us, as we do not use harmful chlorine.




Your benefits:
  • The low salt content makes swimming in our pool a treat for your skin.
  • Your daily swim is not affected by chemicals.
  • If you spend longer in the pool, your skin will wrinkle much less.
  • The water is crystal clear
