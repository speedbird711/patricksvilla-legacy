Beim Einkaufen müssen sie sich in Orlando auf einiges gefasst machen - die Stadt ist der am schnellsten wachsende Einzelhandelsmarkt der USA und es gibt kaum einen besseren Ort, um Shopping zum festen Bestandteil Ihres Urlaubs zu machen.  Florida Mall
8001 South Orange Blossom Trail
Fabulous fashion, exceptional eateries and delightful diversions. That's what shoppers find at Orlando's fabulous fashion destination, Florida Mall. Enjoy delectable treats at each and every turn. Opportunities to relax and refresh the spirit abound and ample parking makes getting here a breeze. Featuring Burdines, Dillard's, JC Penney, Saks Fifth Avenue, Sears, Nordstrom and Lord & Taylor, along with more than 250 specialty stores. Florida Mall offers unparalleled shopping in Central Florida! With a complete renovation and expansion completed in November 1999, Florida Mall boasts more than 50 new specialty retailers with more than 25 of those stores making their Central Florida debut. Come...enjoy...return...to Orlando and Florida Mall - where you'll find simply the best shopping there is. Please call (407) 854-2339 .

 

 Mall at Millenia
4200 Conroy Road
Finally, an Orlando mall for serious shoppers! This one has it all, if upscale, swanky and ritzy is your style. Stores include Bloomingdales, Macy's, Cartier, Luis Vutton, Crate and Barrel, Tommy Bahama, Apple and many more. Located in southwest Orlando near the theme parks and International Drive, Orlando's newest mall is so upscale that it even has a full-service concierge and valet parking ($3). The mall is as beautiful as the merchandise in its stores, with fossilized tile adorning the floors and an 80-foot-long indoor stream gracing the water garden. If you need a relaxing break from the hustle and bustle of shopping, check out the Grand Court, which features 12 giant LED screens airing a variety of entertainment, educational and fashion-related programs. Mall hours are Monday - Saturday from 10am - 9:30pm and Sunday 11am - 7pm. Please call (407) 363-3555 or visit www.mallatmillenia.com.

 

 Orlando Premium Outlet
8200 Vineland Avenue
Bring an extra suitcase! Designer bargains dominate the 50+ designer outlets featured in this mall, operated by Chelsea Premium Outlets. Located near Orlando's major attractions, the Premium Outlets boast the largest collection of designer stores in the area, offering discounts of 25 to 65 percent every day for many of the hottest designers. Outlet hours are Monday-Saturday form 10am - 10pm and Sunday from 10am - 9pm. Please call (407) 238-7787.

 

 The Lake Buena Vista Factory Stores
Located just over 1 mile from Walt Disney World. Take I-4, Exit 68, 2 miles south on State Road 535
Experience relaxed shopping with store front parking.  Select from over 300 designer and name brands.  Stores offer factory direct, quality merchandise at savings up to 75%.  World of Coffee and Reese's Food Court offer specialty coffees, desserts and a wide selection of favorite food items.
