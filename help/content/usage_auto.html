SimpleSecure Usage: Auto Replies

SimpleSecure allows you to send an automatic reply to your visitor when they submit a form. 

You can specify the contents of the auto-reply in the "Auto-Replies" section of the admin panel.  Once you create an auto-reply, you then assign it to a Recipient Record on the Recipient Record page.

The automatic reply will be sent to the person submitting the form, however you MUST use cfgFrom as the Email field on your form.  This is how SimpleSecure knows where to send the automatic reply.

The automatic message text can be any plain text message that you like.  You can additionally specify fields from your form by surrounding them with brackets like so:  {FieldName}.  Any field on your form can be used in, allowing you to customize the message.

As an example, your auto-reply message could look like this:

Dear {FirstName} {LastName}
Thank you for your interest in {ProductName}.
Sincerely,
The Managment

Assuming the fields FirstName, LastName and ProductName appear on your form, those values will be used in the auto-reply.

AutoReply Fields:

The following fields are available for each Auto-Reply Record:

Setting Description
Id This value is incremented automatically.
EmailFormat Format of the message (Text or HTML) 
FromEmail Email from which the auto-reply will be sent.
Subject The subject of the auto-reply message.
Message The contents of the auto-reply message.
